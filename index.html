Started

Goal: 35 children

Founded in 2010 by Joram Muwanguzi and his wife, the orphanage began by caring for about 6 children who had run out of hope in a space they rented to survive. By 2012 the family doubles and from 2014 to date the family has 35 children between the ages of 2 and 18 and continues to grow while supporting other children in the village.

Love + Food + Education

Goal: A decent living for all the childs

Chil Hope Ministries focus on restraint, feeding, education, awareness and treatment of HIV / AIDS (some children have unfortunately inherited the disease and require medical treatment), and the comprehensive well-being of each. Child Hope has, so far, been funded by Joram and his wife. They are now in desperate need of sponsorship as they continue to expand.

Home + School

Goal: Build our own home and school

Today the supreme necessity beyond the funds for food, hygiene, health and education of the children is to finish the construction of the definitive home, since in 2016 it was decided to start building a home of its own, a construction that today stands by Lack of funds, situation that leads children to live in conditions unworthy of life

The Project: The Great Family

Child Hope Ministries is a voluntary, self-funded community organization for non-profit, made up of people with a vision to support, unite and promote the growth of all Ugandans who are in a street situation, with problems such as malnutrition , Diseases such as HIV-AIDS and malaria, lack of love and care.
